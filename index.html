Alamat ng Gubat

A Literary Work by Bob Ong

Introduction

Bob Ong is a Filipino writer and author known for his controversial writing techniques. Throughout his work, the problems and issues of the country are shown by reflecting on the Philippines' life. Ong pursued his passion for writing after dropping out of college. Bob Ong is not his real name. It is a pseudonym that he made when he was working as web designer and teacher.

Bob Ong has published numerous books, six of them having sold millions of copies. Aside from Alamat ng Gubat, his other well-known stories are "ABNKKBSNPLAKo", "Kapitan Sino", "Si", "Lumayo Ka Nga Sa Akin", and other works.

Alamat ng Gubat is the fourth book published by Bob Ong in 2003. The story is written as a humorous and straightforward narrative, depicting Filipinos' everyday lives. However, within the humorous story lies a message about the issues of our country.
